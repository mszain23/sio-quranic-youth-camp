Quiz — The Holy Quran

Section A — The Holy Quran

  1. What was the first word of the Qur'an revealed to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ?
  2. Through which angel did Allah سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ reveal the Holy Qur'an?
  3. Give the name of the cave where the Prophet ﷺ used to meditate and first received revelation.
  4. In which month and year was the Qur'an first revealed to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ?
  5. What is the meaning of the Arabic word "Qur'an"?
  6. Who was the first person the Prophet ﷺ told of his experience of revelation?
  7. What was the age of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ when Allah سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ appointed him His Messenger?
  8. Into how many ajza' (singular: juz') is the Holy Qur'an divided?
  9. How many chapters (surahs) does the Qur'an contain?
  10. Which Khalifah prepared the unified version of the Qur'an?
  11. True or False: Not a single letter or dot has been added or left out of the Qur'an since its revelation.
  12. Give the name of the longest surah of the Qur'an.
  13. Give the names of the shortest surahs containing only three ayat each.
  14. What are the verses of each surah called in Arabic?
  15. In which language was the Qur'an revealed?
  16. Over how many years was the Qur'an revealed?
  17. How many ayat does the Qur'an contain in total?
  18. How many surahs were revealed in Makkah and how many in Madinah?
  19. Which surah of the Qur'an begins without Bismillāhir-Raḥmānir-Raḥīm?
  20. Which surah was completely revealed in one sitting and is also the opening chapter of the Qur'an?

Section B — The Prophets عليهم السلام

  1. Who was the first Prophet عليه السلام and who was the last Prophet ﷺ?
  2. How many Prophets عليهم السلام are mentioned by name in the Qur'an?
  3. Give the Arabic names of the scriptures given to Musa عليه السلام, Dawood عليه السلام, and Eesa عليه السلام.
  4. Which Arabic word is used for the sayings and actions of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ?
  5. Name the five most important Prophets (Ulul 'Azm).
  6. In which year was Prophet Muhammad ﷺ born?
  7. What were the names of the Prophet's ﷺ parents?
  8. What was the name of the wet-nurse who cared for the Prophet ﷺ in early childhood?
  9. The Prophet's ﷺ father passed away before his birth. Who cared for him when his mother also passed away at age six?
  10. In which city of Arabia was Prophet Muhammad ﷺ born?
  11. What is the name of the Prophet's ﷺ tribe?
  12. To which clan of this tribe did the Prophet ﷺ belong?
  13. What is the other name of Madinah?
  14. What is the Islamic calendar year called — beginning from the Hijrah?
  15. What is the Arabic word for the people of Madinah who helped the migrants (Muhajireen)?

  1. Give the Arabic word for the Unity of Allah سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ.
  2. What is the sixth article of faith in Islam?
  3. Name the five pillars of Islam (Arkan ul-Islam).
  4. In which year was fasting during Ramadan made compulsory?
  5. What is the alternative to Wudhu when water is unavailable?
  6. What was the date of the conquest of Makkah?
  7. Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and Khadijah رضي الله عنها had four daughters — can you name them?
  8. Who was the first child to embrace Islam?
  9. Who was the first woman to embrace Islam?
  10. Who was the first man to embrace Islam?
  11. What is the name of the cube-shaped first house built for the worship of Allah سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ?
  12. Who were the first builders of this house?
  13. What is the year called in which Abraha made an unsuccessful attempt to destroy the Ka'bah?
  14. Which surah of the Qur'an describes Abraha's invasion?
  15. At what age does praying five times a day become compulsory for the Muslim?
  16. Two surahs of the Qur'an are named after insects — name them in Arabic and English.
  17. What is the Arabic word used for the biography of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ?
  18. Which was the most important battle of early Islam in the second year of Hijrah?
  19. Who was the first Muadhdhin (caller to prayer) of the Prophet ﷺ?
